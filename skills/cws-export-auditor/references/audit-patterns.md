# Audit Patterns

## Reader audit

Check:

1. Is the headline understandable?
2. Is the scope obvious?
3. Are assumptions clear?
4. Is the evidence basis visible?
5. Is the next action understandable?

## Finance readiness audit

Check:

1. Is projected waste labeled clearly?
2. Are currencies obvious?
3. Are estimates separated from realized savings?
4. Are major cost buckets visible?

## Leadership readiness audit

Check:

1. Is the operating issue summarized clearly?
2. Is the concentration point visible?
3. Is owner/accountability context adequate?
4. Is the decision implication clear?

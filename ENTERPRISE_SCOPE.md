# Enterprise Scope

## Positioning
Enterprise is the centralized control and compliance layer.

It is intended for organizations that need Cloud Waste Scanner to operate across identity, audit, and governance boundaries at scale.

## Included in Enterprise

### Identity and access
- SSO
- SCIM
- Enterprise identity lifecycle integration

### Governance and control
- Centralized policy administration
- Cross-team and cross-instance permission models
- Enterprise-grade audit controls
- Compliance-oriented evidence retention

### Scaled operations
- Centralized reporting across teams or sites
- Controlled sharing mechanisms
- Multi-instance governance coordination
- Security and audit extensions beyond Team workflows

## Not part of Community or Team baseline
- Identity-provider-backed access control
- Enterprise directory sync
- Centralized compliance administration
- Fleet-wide governance rollups

## Product rule
Enterprise should make governance portable across organizational boundaries without weakening local-first evidence integrity.

## Commercial boundary
Enterprise is paid because it solves organization-wide control problems rather than single-team execution problems.
